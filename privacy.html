Privacy

A simpler privacy story because the experience is designed offline

The normal search flow runs against the packaged database inside the app, which keeps the privacy model easier to understand than a live online search portal.

What is searched

The app searches the offline vehicle verification database bundled with the APK.

What is not required

Normal searches do not require a live online connection or an online account.

What you control

You decide when to share, print, copy, or capture a record on your device.

Support contact

If you contact support by email or phone, that communication follows the tools you choose to use outside the app.
