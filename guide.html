User Guide

How to Use This Portal

Follow this step-by-step guide to verify your vehicle's customs clearance status.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Locate Your Vehicle's Chassis Number

The chassis number (also called VIN) is typically found:

  • On the vehicle's registration documents
  • On a metal plate on the dashboard (visible through the windshield)
  • On the driver's side door jamb
  • On the engine block
Tip: Make sure to enter the complete chassis number without spaces or special characters.
2
Enter the Chassis Number

Go to the home page and enter the chassis number in the "Search by Chassis No." field.

Example chassis numbers:
  • MHKM5FA3JKK123456
  • JTEBU5JR9K5123456
  • JTDKB20U983123456
3
Click Search

Click the "Search" button to submit your query. The system will search through customs records for matching vehicles.

4
Review the Results

If a match is found, you'll see the vehicle details including:

  • Customs clearance status
  • Import date
  • Vehicle specifications
  • Customs duty payment information

If no match is found, the vehicle may not have been cleared through customs or there may be an error in the chassis number.

Important Tips

Do's
  • Double-check the chassis number for accuracy
  • Use the combined search if you have multiple identifiers
  • Save or print the verification results for your records
  • Contact customs officials if you have doubts about the results
Don'ts
  • Don't proceed with vehicle purchase if verification fails
  • Don't rely on verbal assurances about customs clearance
  • Don't share your verification results with unauthorized parties
  • Don't use this tool for fraudulent purposes

Alternative Search Methods

If you don't have the chassis number, you can also search using:

  • GD Number: Goods Declaration number from customs
  • IGM Number: Import General Manifest number
  • BL Number: Bill of Lading number
  • ONO Number: Order In Original number (for amnesty cases)
  • Delivery Order Number: (for amnesty cases)

Use the appropriate search form on the home page for these alternative search methods.
