Frequently Asked Questions

If your vehicle is not found in the database, it could mean:
  • The vehicle has not completed customs clearance
  • The vehicle was imported through unofficial channels
  • There might be an error in the chassis number you entered
  • The vehicle's data has not been updated in the system yet
We recommend double-checking the chassis number and trying again. If the problem persists, contact customs officials for clarification.

This tool uses publicly available data from Pakistan Customs for verification purposes. While it provides accurate information based on customs records, it is not the official government website. For official verification, please contact the Collectorate of Customs directly.

The data is updated regularly based on Pakistan Customs records. The current database is updated till 15 OCT 2023 as indicated on the homepage. We strive to keep the information as current as possible, but there may be a delay between customs processing and database updates.

We value your privacy. Search queries are not stored permanently on our servers. The verification process is anonymous, and we do not collect personal information during searches. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

While we strive for accuracy, the verification results should be used as a reference rather than absolute proof. There may be rare cases of data discrepancies or delays in updates. For critical decisions like vehicle purchases, we recommend cross-verifying with official customs channels.

If verification fails:
  1. Double-check the chassis number for accuracy
  2. Try alternative search methods (GD number, IGM number, etc.)
  3. Contact the seller for clarification
  4. Reach out to customs officials for official verification
Do not proceed with the purchase until you have confirmed the vehicle's legal status.

For verification through this portal, you typically need:
  • Vehicle chassis number (VIN)
  • Or GD number (Goods Declaration)
  • Or IGM number (Import General Manifest)
  • Or BL number (Bill of Lading)
These identifiers can usually be found on your vehicle's import documents or registration papers.

No, this verification service is provided free of charge to help citizens verify the customs status of vehicles. There are no hidden fees or charges for using this portal.
