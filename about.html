About The App

A Pakistan-focused offline verification experience for vehicle customs checks

PRAAL Offline is built to make customs-linked vehicle verification clearer on Android, especially when users are working in the field, comparing documents, or reviewing a potential vehicle purchase.

Why it exists

The app packages the offline database directly into the Android build so users can search without depending on a live web connection.

What it covers

Search methods include chassis, GD, IGM, BL, ONO, delivery order, and a combined multi-field route for partial paperwork.

Design direction

This refreshed version uses Pakistan Customs-inspired color, stronger typography, cleaner cards, and a more polished mobile-first layout.

Important note

The app is excellent for reference and review, but official customs channels remain the right path for disputes, enforcement, and legal confirmation.
